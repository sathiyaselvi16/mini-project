The 2024 BMW i7 is a luxury electric sedan that comes in various versions, offering a blend of performance, range, and high-end features. Performance: The i7 xDrive60, with its dual electric motors (one for each axle), delivers 536 horsepower and 549 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 4.3 seconds​ INSIDEEVS . The performance-focused i7 M70 produces 650 horsepower, and its rear motor is significantly more powerful, providing 748 lb-ft of torque in Sport mode. It can reach 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds​ EDMUNDS . Range and Efficiency: The i7 xDrive60 offers a range between 296 to 318 miles on a single charge, depending on the wheel size​ EDMUNDS ​ INSIDEEVS . The i7 M70, with a performance-driven setup, has an estimated range of 295 miles​ INSIDEEVS . Charging: The i7 supports DC fast charging, with a 10-80% charge taking about 34 minutes at a charging rate of 195 kW​ INSIDEEVS . Interior and Features: The i7's cabin is designed for luxury, with options like cashmere seating and a futuristic dashboard with dual curved screens​ EDMUNDS . It boasts a 31-inch 8K rear entertainment screen with Amazon Fire TV, providing an immersive experience for rear passengers​
