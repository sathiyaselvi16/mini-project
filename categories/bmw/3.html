The 2024 BMW i4 is a fully electric luxury sedan with a sleek design and advanced features. It offers up to 500 km of range (WLTP) and a real-world range of around 430 km, depending on conditions. The car is available in multiple variants, including the eDrive35 and eDrive40, featuring rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. Performance-wise, it can accelerate from 0-100 km/h in as little as 3.9 seconds in the M50 variant. The i4 starts at approximately ₹72 lakh in India
