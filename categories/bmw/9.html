iX

$77,800

The BMW iX is an all-electric luxury SUV, with the 2024 model offering a high-tech and high-performance experience. It comes in multiple trims, including the xDrive50 and the M60, with both featuring all-wheel drive. The base xDrive50 generates 516 horsepower, while the M60 produces an impressive 610 horsepower. The iX has a strong electric range, offering between 274 to 324 miles per charge, depending on the trim and configuration. Key features include a 14.9-inch touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, quad-zone climate control, hands-free parking assist, and advanced driver assistance systems like blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert. The M60 trim comes with additional performance features, including the M Sport suspension. Pricing starts at $87,100 for the base iX xDrive50, with the iX M60 starting at $111,500. The iX competes with other luxury EVs such as the Audi Q8 e-Tron, Cadillac Lyriq, and Tesla Model X.
