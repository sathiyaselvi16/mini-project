iX2

$79,990

The 2024 BMW iX2 is an all-electric compact SUV, available in two main variants: the iX2 eDrive20 and the iX2 xDrive30. The eDrive20 features a single front-mounted motor producing 150 kW of power and 247 Nm of torque, while the xDrive30 boasts a dual-motor all-wheel-drive setup with 230 kW and 494 Nm, capable of accelerating from 0-100 km/h in just 5.6 seconds. In terms of range, the iX2 eDrive20 offers up to 477 km on a full charge (WLTP), and the iX2 xDrive30 offers a range of up to 449 km. Both versions can be fast-charged using DC chargers at up to 130 kW, which takes the battery from 10% to 80% in under 30 minutes. The iX2 supports AC charging up to 22 kW as well. The car's dimensions include a length of 4554 mm, width of 1845 mm, and height of 1560 mm, with a wheelbase of 2692 mm. It provides a boot capacity of 525 liters, which expands to 1400 liters with the rear seats folded down. The iX2 is equipped with various safety features like adaptive cruise control, lane assist, blind-spot monitoring, and a parking assistant, including surround-view cameras. The car also includes advanced technology such as a 10.25-inch infotainment system and a head-up display. Pricing in Australia starts at $82,900 for the eDrive20 and $85,700 for the xDrive30 (before on-road costs).
