The 2024 BMW XM is a luxury plug-in hybrid SUV with powerful performance and cutting-edge technology. Key details include: Performance: Engine: A 4.4L twin-turbocharged V8 paired with an electric motor. Power: 644 hp (standard trim) or up to 738 hp in the Label Red version. Electric Range: Approximately 50 km on a full charge. 0-100 km/h: Achieved in 4.3 seconds. Top Speed: Up to 270 km/h. Design: Interior: Offers a premium experience with features like ventilated seats, a head-up display, and advanced driver-assistance systems. Exterior: Bold and polarizing design with 22-inch wheels as standard. Features: Advanced infotainment system compatible with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. Hybrid system includes a 29.5 kWh lithium-ion battery with fast charging capabilities (about 3.2 hours at 240V). Pricing: Base Model: Starts at $220,000. Label Red Version: Priced at approximately $259,000​
