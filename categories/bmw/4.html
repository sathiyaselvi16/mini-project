The 2024 BMW i5 is an all-electric luxury sedan, available in two trims: i5 eDrive40 and i5 M60 xDrive. - **i5 eDrive40**: It features a single electric motor with rear-wheel drive, producing 335 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in 5.7 seconds. The standard range is up to 295 miles on a single charge with 19-inch wheels, achieving a combined fuel economy of up to 105 MPGe. - **i5 M60 xDrive**: The M60 comes with dual electric motors and all-wheel drive, producing 590 horsepower and 586 lb-ft of torque, reaching 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds. This version has a range of up to 256 miles on a full charge with 19-inch wheels and a combined fuel economy of 91 MPGe. The i5 also comes with advanced safety features like forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control. Additional safety and comfort features are available in premium packages. Prices for the 2024 BMW i5 range from **$67,795** for the eDrive40 to **$85,095** for the M60 xDrive.
