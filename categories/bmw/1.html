The 2024 BMW Z4 is a luxury sports car available in two trims: the sDrive30i and the M40i. Here's an overview of its key features and pricing: Pricing: sDrive30i: Starts at $54,595. M40i: Starts at $67,295, offering more power and additional features. Performance: sDrive30i: Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine with 255 horsepower, accelerating from 0-60 mph in 5.2 seconds. M40i: Equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine delivering 382 horsepower, achieving 0-60 mph in 3.9 seconds. Features: A retractable soft top that operates in 10 seconds at speeds up to 30 mph. Advanced driver-assistance systems, including blind spot detection and lane departure warning. Premium interior with high-quality materials and tech such as the iDrive 7 infotainment system. Optional Harman Kardon sound system with 12 speakers. Fuel Economy: sDrive30i: 28 MPG combined (25 city, 33 highway). M40i: 26 MPG combined.
