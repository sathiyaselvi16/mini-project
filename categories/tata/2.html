The Tata Tigor is a stylish compact sedan offering a variety of features and engine options. It comes in both petrol and CNG variants, with the engine size being 1199 cc. The price of the Tata Tigor ranges between ₹6 lakh and ₹9.4 lakh (ex-showroom), depending on the model and configuration. It boasts of features like LED taillights, projector headlamps, and chrome accents, along with comfortable leatherette seats and a spacious rear seat for a comfortable ride. The Tata Tigor is equipped with a 1.2-liter engine delivering between 72.4 and 84.5 bhp, and offers a mileage range of 18.8 to 20.09 kmpl for petrol variants, with higher fuel efficiency for CNG versions. It also has a boot space of 419 liters, making it a practical option for daily use.
