Tata Harrier

$85,095

The Tata Harrier is a stylish and powerful SUV, recently updated with significant design and feature improvements. Launched in India on 17th October 2023, the Harrier offers a bold and modern aesthetic with redesigned LED headlights, a new grille, and LED DRLs. It is equipped with features like a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, digital instrument cluster, and a premium cabin with dual-tone interiors and ambient lighting. The SUV is powered by a 2.0-liter Kryotec diesel engine, delivering 168 bhp and 350 Nm of torque. It offers both manual and automatic transmission options, with fuel efficiency up to 16.8 km/l. Safety features are also a highlight, with the Harrier achieving a 5-star GNCAP safety rating. Pricing for the Harrier starts at around ₹14.99 lakh and can go up to ₹25.89 lakh, depending on the variant, which includes options like Smart, Pure, and Adventure.
