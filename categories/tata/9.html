Tata Nexon EV

₹6.49 lakh

The Tata Nexon EV is a popular electric SUV offering a combination of style, performance, and affordability. The price starts at ₹12.49 lakh and can go up to ₹17.19 lakh depending on the variant. It is available in several configurations, with the driving range varying from 275 km to 489 km based on the battery and model selected. Some of the key features of the Tata Nexon EV include: - **Battery Capacity**: It comes with options of 30 kWh, 40.5 kWh, or 46.08 kWh, providing varying ranges. - **Power and Performance**: It offers power outputs from 127 bhp up to 148 bhp with acceleration from 0-100 km/h in under 10 seconds. - **Safety**: The Nexon EV includes multiple airbags, electronic stability control (ESC), ABS with EBD, and robust crash protection. - **Comfort and Convenience**: It has a spacious 5-seater cabin, ventilated front seats, cruise control, and a premium JBL sound system. - **Charging and Efficiency**: It supports home and public charging, with costs as low as ₹0.96 per km depending on your charging method. If you're looking for an eco-friendly, feature-packed electric SUV, the Tata Nexon EV is a great choice, especially with its range, performance, and technology at this price point.
