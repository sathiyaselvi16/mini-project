Tata Safari

₹5.82 lakh

The Tata Safari is a popular 7-seater SUV, available in both manual and automatic transmission options. It is powered by a 1956 cc diesel engine and has a mileage ranging from 14.08 to 16.14 km per liter, depending on the variant. In terms of variants, the Tata Safari comes in several trim options, including Pure, Smart, Adventure, Accomplished, and more, with a price range of ₹15.49 lakh to ₹26.79 lakh (ex-showroom). The car also comes with several advanced features, including driver assistance systems like lane-keep assist and adaptive steering assist. The Safari is available in a range of attractive colors, such as Stellar Frost, Oberon Black, Lunar Slate, and Supernova Copper. It is designed for comfort with spacious seating for up to 7 passengers.
