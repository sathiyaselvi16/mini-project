Tata Tiago

$79,990

The Tata Tiago is a compact hatchback that is known for its affordability, style, and strong safety ratings. It's available in both petrol and petrol+CNG variants, with a 1199cc engine. The Tiago offers good fuel efficiency, with the petrol version providing around 20 kmpl and the CNG version offering about 26.4 km/kg. The Tiago's pricing ranges from approximately ₹5.45 lakh to ₹8.05 lakh (ex-showroom), depending on the variant. The car is available in multiple variants, including XE, XM, XT, XZ+, and others, with features like a Harman audio system, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a quick-cooling A/C. Additionally, the Tiago holds a 4-star safety rating from Global NCAP, making it a safe choice for families. The Tiago's design is modern, and it comes in a variety of colors, with options for both manual and automatic transmissions. There are also multiple CNG variants for those seeking higher fuel efficiency.
