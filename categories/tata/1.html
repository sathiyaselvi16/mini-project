Tata Altroz

£22,378 to £24,009

The Tata Altroz is a premium hatchback that is available in several variants, including petrol, diesel, and CNG options. The prices for the Tata Altroz range from ₹6.65 lakh to ₹10.99 lakh (ex-showroom), depending on the variant you choose. Some of the key highlights include: - **Engine Options**: A 1.2-litre petrol engine with 88 PS, a turbocharged 1.2-litre engine in the Altroz Racer (120 PS), and a 1.5-litre diesel engine (90 PS). The car is available with both manual and automatic transmissions, including a dual-clutch automatic for the petrol variant. - **Features**: The Altroz comes with advanced tech like a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, a 7-inch digital driver's display, a sunroof, cruise control, and an 8-speaker Harman sound system. Safety features include 6 airbags, ABS with EBD, and a 360-degree camera. - **Mileage**: It offers fuel efficiency ranging from 18.5 to 23.64 km/l, depending on the fuel type and variant. The Tata Altroz competes with rivals like the Maruti Suzuki Baleno, Hyundai i20, and Toyota Glanza.
