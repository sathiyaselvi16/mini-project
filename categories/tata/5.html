The Tata Punch is a popular compact SUV in India, designed to provide a blend of style, space, and practicality. It is available in various trims, including both petrol and CNG variants. Key Features of Tata Punch: - Engine: 1.2L, 3-cylinder petrol engine (1199 cc), with a mileage of around 18.8 to 20.09 kmpl for petrol and 26.99 km/kg for the CNG version. - Transmission: Available in manual and automatic options (AMT). - Dimensions: Compact design suitable for urban driving, with a height that makes it feel more like an SUV than a typical hatchback. - Interior: Includes a 7-inch or 10.25-inch touchscreen, wireless phone charger, auto AC, and rear parking camera. - Safety: The Punch has received high praise for its safety features, including a strong build and standard features like dual airbags, ABS, and rear parking sensors.
