Q8 e-tron Sportback

$77,800

The 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron Sportback is a luxury electric SUV with a sleek coupe-like design. It offers an EPA-estimated range of up to 300 miles per charge, depending on conditions. Features include adaptive air suspension for optimized comfort, progressive steering, and advanced technology such as the Audi Virtual Cockpit Plus with a 12.3-inch HD display. It supports fast charging with Plug&Charge at Electrify America stations, and buyers receive two years of free charging at these stations. For pricing, the base starts around **$77,800** in the U.S., with options increasing the cost.
