Q8 e-tron

$74,400

The 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron is a premium electric SUV with an advanced powertrain offering up to 300 miles of range. It features Audi's signature quattro all-wheel drive, adaptive air suspension, and luxurious interiors with high-tech enhancements like the Audi Virtual Cockpit Plus and Bang & Olufsen sound system. Charging is efficient with DC fast charging and Plug&Charge functionality at Electrify America stations. Prices start at approximately $74,400 for the standard model and $77,800 for the Sportback variant. For more details, visit Audi's official site.
