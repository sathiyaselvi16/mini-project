The **2024 Maruti Suzuki Swift** is a stylish and feature-rich hatchback that blends sporty aesthetics with practical functionality. Here's a detailed overview: ### Key Specifications: - **Price**: Starts at ₹6.49 lakh (ex-showroom, India). - **Engine**: 1.2L DualJet petrol engine, delivering 81.5 PS at 5700 rpm and 111.7 Nm of torque at 4300 rpm. - **Transmission**: Available in manual (MT) and automatic (AMT) options. - **Mileage**: Impressive fuel efficiency of 24-25.75 km/l. - **Seating Capacity**: 5 seats. - **Dimensions**: Length - 3860 mm, Width - 1735 mm, Height - 1520 mm, Ground Clearance - 163 mm. ### Features: - **Infotainment**: Options include SmartPlay Pro (7-inch) or SmartPlay Pro Plus (9-inch) touchscreens. - **Safety**: Dual airbags, ABS with EBD, rear parking sensors, and ESP with hill hold assist on AMT variants. - **Lighting**: LED projector headlamps and LED tail lamps. - **Suspension**: Front - MacPherson strut, Rear - Torsion beam. ### Variants and Colors: - **Variants**: LXi, VXi, ZXi, ZXi+, with CNG options in select trims. - **Colors**: Multiple choices, including dual-tone options like Sizzling Red with Pearl Midnight Black Roof and Luster Blue. The Swift is highly appreciated for its peppy performance, reliable build, and modern features, making it a top choice in the hatchback segment.
