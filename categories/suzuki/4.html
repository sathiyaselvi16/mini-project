The Maruti S-Presso is a compact, stylish car designed for city driving. It offers a bold SUV-inspired design and features a spacious interior. It is equipped with a 998 cc engine, available in both petrol and CNG variants. The car's performance is efficient, offering a top speed of 148 km/h and an average mileage of around 19-24 km/l, depending on the driving conditions. Key features include air conditioning, power steering, rear parking sensors, and safety features like ABS and airbags for both the driver and passenger. The S-Presso is available in several variants, starting from the basic STD model priced at Rs. 4.26 Lakh and going up to Rs. 5.95 Lakh for the higher variants like the VXi Plus Opt AT.
