Brezza

₹8.34 lakh

The Maruti Suzuki Brezza is a compact SUV available in both petrol and CNG variants, with a starting price of ₹8.34 lakh (ex-showroom) and going up to ₹13.98 lakh for higher trims. It offers a mileage range of 17.38 to 25.51 kmpl, depending on the variant. The Brezza comes with a 1462cc engine and is available with manual and automatic transmission options​ V3CARS ​ SMARTPRIX . Key features include: 5-seater capacity Automatic climate control Rear AC vents LED headlamps and tail lamps 6 airbags for safety Connectivity options like wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, as well as wireless charging​
