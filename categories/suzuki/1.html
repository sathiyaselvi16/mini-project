The Maruti Suzuki Ertiga is a versatile 7-seater MPV, available in both petrol and CNG variants. The car offers good fuel efficiency, with the petrol variant providing a mileage of around 20.3 to 20.5 km/l, while the CNG version delivers an impressive 26.1 km/kg. In terms of pricing, the base petrol variant, the Ertiga LXi (O), starts at approximately ₹8.7 lakh, and the top petrol variant, the Ertiga ZXi Plus AT, is priced around ₹13.0 lakh. The CNG variants start at about ₹9.8 lakh.
