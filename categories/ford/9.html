The 2024 Ford Kuga offers a refined design, advanced technology, and multiple powertrain options. It features a more premium interior with a redesigned dashboard and upgraded infotainment system, including a standard 8-inch screen or an optional 12.3-inch screen with SYNC 4. The system supports smartphone connectivity, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and even Alexa Built-In for voice commands. Under the hood, the Kuga offers several powertrain choices: - A 1.5L EcoBoost petrol engine with 150 horsepower. - A 2.0L EcoBoost petrol engine with 190 horsepower. - A mild hybrid (mHEV) and a plug-in hybrid (PHEV) version, the latter combining a 2.5L Duratec petrol engine with an electric motor to deliver 225 horsepower and up to 56 km of electric-only driving range. Key features include advanced driver-assistance technologies like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a 360-degree camera system. The Kuga also provides excellent practicality, with sliding second-row seats to increase legroom or boost boot space. The Kuga comes in various trims like Titanium, ST-Line, and Active, catering to different driving needs, from city commuting to rugged off-road adventures.
