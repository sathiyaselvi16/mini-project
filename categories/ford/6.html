The 2024 Ford Edge is a stylish and spacious five-passenger SUV that offers a range of impressive features and performance. Available in multiple trims, including SE, SEL, ST-Line, Titanium, and ST, the Edge combines bold exterior styling with a modern, tech-forward interior. **Engine Options and Performance:** - The base model comes with a 2.0-liter EcoBoost turbocharged four-cylinder engine, producing 250 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. - The performance-oriented ST trim features a more powerful 2.7-liter V6 engine, making it ideal for those who want a spirited drive. **Fuel Economy:** - The Edge offers excellent fuel efficiency, with an estimated 21 MPG in the city and 28 MPG on the highway. **Interior and Technology:** - The cabin is designed for comfort with high-tech features, including Ford’s SYNC 4A infotainment system, which includes voice-activated navigation and real-time traffic updates. The SUV also offers FordPass Connect for in-vehicle Wi-Fi. **Pricing:** - Prices for the 2024 Ford Edge start at around $40,505 for the SE trim, with the top-tier ST trim nearing $50,000 before optional add-ons.
