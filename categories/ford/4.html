The **2024 Ford Bronco** is a versatile and off-road-capable mid-size SUV with options for two- or four-door configurations and a range of trim levels. Here's an overview of its key features and pricing: ### Engine and Performance - **Base Engine**: 2.3L turbocharged four-cylinder, producing 300 horsepower and 315 lb-ft of torque, with a choice between a 7-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission. - **Optional Engine**: 2.7L twin-turbo V6 offering 330 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque. - **Raptor Trim**: Equipped with a 418-horsepower 3.0L twin-turbo V6 for high-performance desert racing and extreme off-roading. ### Key Features - Standard four-wheel drive across all trims. - Removable doors and roof for open-air driving. - Various off-road packages, including skid plates, rock rails, and locking differentials. - Newly standard 12-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. ### Trims and Pricing (Approximate Starting Prices): 1. **Big Bend**: Base trim starting around $41,000. 2. **Everglades**: Focused on wet conditions, starting at $57,415. 3. **Wildtrak**: Equipped for advanced off-road driving, starting at $61,920. 4. **Heritage Limited Edition**: A retro-inspired model, starting at $71,580. 5. **Raptor**: The ultimate off-road trim, starting at $91,730. ### Pros - Exceptional off-road capabilities. - Customizable options for tops, doors, and off-road features. - Modern infotainment and safety features. ### Cons - Subpar fuel economy (15-21 MPG depending on configuration). - Cramped rear seats in the two-door model. - Higher trim prices can climb steeply.
