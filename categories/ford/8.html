The 2024 Ford Escape is available in multiple trims, including Active, ST-Line, ST-Line Select, ST-Line Elite, and Platinum. It features options for both gasoline and hybrid powertrains, with the standard 1.5L 3-cylinder engine delivering 181 horsepower and the available 2.0L turbocharged engine producing 250 horsepower. The hybrid and plug-in hybrid models offer combined system horsepower of 192 and 210, respectively. For towing, the Escape can pull up to 3,500 pounds when properly equipped with the 2.0L EcoBoost engine and the Class II Trailer Tow Package. Inside, the Escape boasts an advanced infotainment system with a 13.2" touchscreen on higher trims, along with Ford Co-Pilot360 Assist 2.0 for enhanced safety features, including adaptive cruise control and evasive steering. Pricing for the 2024 Ford Escape starts around $30,990, but special discounts and offers can reduce the price significantly. For more details, you can visit Ford's official site or a nearby dealership for availability and specific offers.
