Capri EV

£48,000

The new Ford Capri EV is a modern, all-electric SUV coupe that pays homage to the original Ford Capri sports car, which was a popular model in the late 1960s. This 2024 version features an electric powertrain and sleek, coupe-like styling, marking a shift from the classic gas-powered model. The Capri EV offers a range of up to 390 miles (627 km) with its 77 kWh battery, and the top-end version with an AWD drivetrain boasts 335 horsepower. It can accelerate from 0 to 62 mph (100 km/h) in just 5.3 seconds. Inside, the Capri EV combines a minimalist design with advanced technology, including a 14.6-inch adjustable infotainment screen, Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, and a spacious 527-liter boot. The vehicle comes in two trims: a rear-wheel-drive model with 282 horsepower and the all-wheel-drive version with 335 horsepower. Ford expects the Capri EV to be priced starting around £48,000 (approximately $50,000), with a potential cheaper version available in 2025.
